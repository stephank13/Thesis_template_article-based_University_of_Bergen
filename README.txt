Introduction
============

Sample latex project for PhD dissertations based on an earlier version made by Tore Birkeland and Raymond Nepstad, modified and made available by Kjersti Birkeland Daae and Stephan Kral. This is not an official document but it may be accepted by your faculty and the University of Bergen. Note that the outlined structure is just an example you are not obliged to stick to. Visit https://avhandling.uib.no/en/ for official information on the thesis layout and requirements. For feedback please contact stephan.kral@uib.no or kjersti.daae@uib.no

working offline:
=========
Please follow the overleaf instructions on working offline: https://www.overleaf.com/learn/how-to/Working_Offline_in_Overleaf#.W6oPIBRoTmE and https://www.overleaf.com/learn/how-to/How_does_Overleaf_compile_my_project%3F for information on how to compile the project.
